FAQ

Does the extension collect personal or payment data?
No. All processing happens locally in your browser using data already loaded on the Stripe Dashboard page. We do not collect or transmit personal or payment data.

What analytics are used?
Anonymous technical telemetry only (e.g., extension version, session ID, page type, feature usage, decline detection events). See our Privacy Policy for details.
